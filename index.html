About

Sparkling in brilliance
since 2023.

5 Fish was founded with a clear vision: to build a practice that combines financial acumen with genuine community impact. Based in Rogers Park, we take a thoughtful approach to investment and advisory work.

Our name—“five fish”—anchors us in the rhythms of flow, resilience, and intentional growth — the idea that success is not a zero-sum game, but something that can multiply when approached with intention and generosity.

Whether supporting causes close to our heart, advising enterprise clients through complex transformations, or simply creating space for reflection by the lake, 5 Fish operates with purpose at every level.
